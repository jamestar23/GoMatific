About GoMatific

An eLearning Mobile application exclusively for 5th Grader of Cubal Elementary School.
Developed by IT students of Philippine College of Northwestern Luzon for fullfilment of Thesis Project.

Features

Distributed Lessons (Chapters 1, 2, ,3 and 4) Search Lesson Math Dictionary (Mathtionary) Math Game (Question and Answer) Video Tutorials (requires Internet Connection)

User Interface

Press "Start" to go to Homepage Homepage functions:    Search Button: Brings you to Search page where you can search lesson    Chapters Button: Gives you a guide for choosing a lesson in certain quarter    Watch Videos Button: To view all the video tutorials related to Grade 5 math lessons    Play Game Button: To play Q&A related to Lessons App Tabs:     Home : Most of the Features are in this page     Mathtionary : Math Definition of terms     About : Information and Guides about this App Special Thanks to Kisi KidsMathTV for Video Tutorials     Channel link: https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCJuvQVOB9k1sj6nDwIDj4CQ
